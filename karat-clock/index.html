United States
The global benchmark. COMEX futures in New York set the world spot price. No import duty on gold bullion. Deepest liquidity, tightest spreads. The reference price everything else is measured against.
COMEX Benchmark 0% Import Duty Tightest Spreads

China
The world's largest gold consumer and producer. The Shanghai Gold Exchange (SGE) sets domestic prices. Capital controls and yuan conversion friction create a consistent premium over London/COMEX spot, typically $5–$30/oz on gold.
SGE Exchange Capital Controls ~$5–30 Premium

India
The world's second-largest gold consumer, driven by jewelry demand and cultural tradition. India levies a 15% import duty on gold bullion plus GST, creating the largest consistent premium of the three markets — often $40–$80/oz above spot.
15% Import Duty + 3% GST ~$40–80 Premium
About KaratClock
LAST UPDATED · APRIL 2026

KaratClock is an independent precious metals price tracking platform built for investors, collectors, and anyone curious about the real-time value of gold, silver, platinum, and palladium.

Our Mission

We believe everyone deserves access to transparent, real-time precious metals pricing — without paywalls, subscriptions, or confusing financial jargon. KaratClock makes it simple to track spot prices, compare products across trusted retailers, and understand how global markets affect the price of the metals you care about.

What We Offer

  • Live spot prices for gold, silver, platinum and palladium updated every 60 seconds
  • Product price comparison across JM Bullion, APMEX, and Kitco
  • Global market comparison — USA, China, and India side by side
  • Premium-over-spot analysis to help you find the best deal

How We Make Money

KaratClock is free to use. We earn a small referral commission from our retail partners when you click through and make a purchase. This never affects the prices you see or the objectivity of our comparisons — you always pay the same price as going direct.

Data Sources

Spot prices are sourced in real time via gold-api.com. Product premiums are based on current published retail pricing from each listed retailer and are updated regularly. Always verify prices directly with the retailer before purchasing.

KaratClock is an independent website and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by JM Bullion, APMEX, or Kitco.
