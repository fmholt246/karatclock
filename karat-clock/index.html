About KaratClock
LAST UPDATED · APRIL 2026

KaratClock is an independent precious metals price tracking platform built for investors, collectors, and anyone curious about the real-time value of gold, silver, platinum, and palladium.

Our Mission

We believe everyone deserves access to transparent, real-time precious metals pricing — without paywalls, subscriptions, or confusing financial jargon.

What We Offer

  • Live spot prices for gold, silver, platinum and palladium updated every 60 seconds
  • Product price comparison across JM Bullion, APMEX, and Kitco
  • Global market comparison — USA, China, and India side by side
  • Premium-over-spot analysis to help you find the best deal

How We Make Money

KaratClock is free to use. We earn a small referral commission from our retail partners when you click through and make a purchase. This never affects the prices you see or the objectivity of our comparisons.

Data Sources

Spot prices are sourced in real time via gold-api.com. Product premiums are based on current published retail pricing and are updated regularly. Always verify prices directly with the retailer before purchasing.

KaratClock is an independent website and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by JM Bullion, APMEX, or Kitco.
